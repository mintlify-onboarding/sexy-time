---
title: "Customer Support"
---

You can integrate Intercom and Frontchat onto your docs for customer support. Add the `integrations` field into your `mint.json` file with your respective app ID.

```json
  "integrations": {
    "intercom": "abcdefz",
    "frontchat": "abcdez"
  }
```

If you'd like to request a customer support integration, please join [our community](https://join.slack.com/t/mintlify-users/shared_invite/zt-1xfzz6x35-f4o4WCYfpvLhSj3O7WAOMA).
